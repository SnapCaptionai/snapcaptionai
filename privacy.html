SnapCaption ("the App") is an AI-powered content creation tool built for creators. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Usage data stored locally on your device only: generations, streaks, saved content, preferences. This data never leaves your device.
  • Content you enter is sent to AI APIs (Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic Claude) to generate results. We do not store this on our servers.
  • Email address (optional) — if you enter your email for bonus generations, it is stored securely via Kit (kit.com). You can unsubscribe anytime.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name unless provided
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not share data with advertisers
  • We do not store generated content on any server

3. Third-Party AI Services

Your prompts are sent to Google Gemini (policies.google.com/privacy), OpenAI (openai.com/privacy), or Anthropic Claude (anthropic.com/privacy) depending on availability. These services process your input to generate content.

4. Email Marketing

SnapCaption uses Kit (kit.com) to manage email communications. We never sell your email. Unsubscribe anytime.

5. Advertising

The free version may display ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve relevant ads. Pro users are completely ad-free.

6. Children's Privacy

SnapCaption is for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Clear all local data anytime via Settings → Clear All Saved Data. To request deletion of any other data contact us at hellosnapcaptionai@gmail.com.
