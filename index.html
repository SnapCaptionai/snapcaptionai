How To Use
Get the most out of SnapCaption in 5 steps.
1
Set Up Your Voice Profile
Go to Settings → Voice & Profile. Add your tone, niche, and brand. Every tool will automatically write in YOUR style — not generic AI text.
2
Start with Quick Create
On the home screen, drop any idea into Quick Create. In seconds you get a hook, caption, CTA, and hashtags — ready to post.
3
Use the Refine Buttons
Every result has Shorter, Rewrite, More Dramatic, and More Human buttons. Keep refining until it sounds exactly like you. No limits on refines.
4
Build Your 30-Day Calendar
Go to the Calendar tool and fill in your business details. SnapCaption builds a full 30-day content path that follows a sales strategy — not just random post ideas.
5
Save and Copy Your Best Content
Hit the ⭐ Save button on any result. Find all your saved content in the Saved tab. Copy and paste directly into Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, or LinkedIn.

Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026 · Developer: Shatica R Miles

SnapCaption ("the App") is an AI-powered content creation tool built for creators. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

Usage data stored locally on your device only: generations, streaks, saved content, preferences. This data never leaves your device.

Content you enter is sent to AI APIs (Google Gemini or OpenAI) to generate results. We do not store this on our servers.

Email address (optional) — if you enter your email for bonus generations, it is stored securely via Kit (kit.com). You can unsubscribe anytime.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name unless provided. We do not track location. We do not share data with advertisers. We do not store generated content on any server.

3. Third-Party AI Services

Your prompts are sent to Google Gemini (policies.google.com/privacy) or OpenAI (openai.com/privacy) depending on your settings.

4. Email Marketing

SnapCaption uses Kit (kit.com) to manage email communications. We never sell your email. Unsubscribe anytime.

5. Advertising

The free version may display ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve relevant ads. Pro users are completely ad-free.

6. Children's Privacy

SnapCaption is for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Clear all local data anytime via Settings → Clear All Saved Data. To request deletion of any other data contact us at hellosnapcaptionai@gmail.com.
